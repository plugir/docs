General Settings

The General Settings tab allows you to configure the core content protection features provided by the ContentGuard plugin. These settings enable you to safeguard your website's content against unauthorized access, copying, and manipulation.

Features​

  1. Disable Right Click:

    • Prevents users from right-clicking on the page, stopping them from accessing the context menu to copy images or text.

  2. Disable Text Selection:

    • Stops users from selecting text on the page, ensuring that your content cannot be easily copied.

  3. Disable Copy Shortcut (Ctrl+C):

    • Blocks the Ctrl+C keyboard shortcut, making it difficult for users to copy text using keyboard commands.

  4. Disable Print Screen & Detect Screen Capture:

    • Prevents users from taking screenshots of your content, ensuring that your site’s visuals remain secure.

  5. Disable Drag and Drop:

    • Prevents users from dragging and dropping content, maintaining the integrity of your site's design.

  6. Protect Images:

    • Disables right-click and drag actions on images, protecting your visual content from unauthorized use.

  7. Disable Source View:

    • Prevents users from viewing the page source, keeping your site's code hidden and secure.

  8. Disable Developer Tools:

    • Attempts to disable or limit access to browser developer tools, making it more difficult for users to inspect or manipulate your site's code.

  9. Disable Clipboard Access:

    • Stops unauthorized copying by blocking clipboard access, ensuring no content can be copied and pasted.

  10. Disable Save Page:

    • Blocks the ability to save the entire webpage, maintaining control over your site's content distribution.

  11. Disable WebRTC:

    • Prevents WebRTC leaks which can reveal the user’s IP address, protecting user privacy.

  12. Disable Print:

    • Prevents users from printing the page, ensuring your content stays online.

  13. Disable Reader Mode:

    • Prevents users from enabling reader mode, ensuring they view your content as intended.

How It Works​

The settings you configure in the General Settings tab will apply globally across your site, providing a comprehensive layer of protection for your content. Each feature is designed to target specific methods of unauthorized content access or copying.

Using the General Settings​

  1. Disable Right Click:

    • Toggle the switch to enable or disable right-click prevention on your site. This stops users from accessing the context menu to copy images or text.

  2. Disable Text Selection:

    • Toggle the switch to enable or disable text selection prevention, ensuring that users cannot highlight and copy your content.

  3. Disable Copy Shortcut (Ctrl+C):

    • Toggle the switch to block the Ctrl+C keyboard shortcut, making it more difficult for users to copy text using keyboard commands.

  4. Disable Print Screen & Detect Screen Capture:

    • Toggle the switch to prevent users from taking screenshots or using screen capture tools.

  5. Disable Drag and Drop:

    • Toggle the switch to prevent users from dragging and dropping content on your site, preserving your site's design integrity.

  6. Protect Images:

    • Toggle the switch to disable right-click and drag actions on images, protecting your visual content from unauthorized use.

  7. Disable Source View:

    • Toggle the switch to prevent users from viewing the page source, adding an extra layer of security to your site's code.

  8. Disable Developer Tools:

    • Toggle the switch to limit access to browser developer tools, making it more difficult for users to inspect or manipulate your site's code.

  9. Disable Clipboard Access:

    • Toggle the switch to stop unauthorized copying by blocking clipboard access.

  10. Disable Save Page:

    • Toggle the switch to block the ability to save the entire webpage.

  11. Disable WebRTC:

    • Toggle the switch to prevent WebRTC leaks, protecting user privacy.

  12. Disable Print:

    • Toggle the switch to prevent users from printing the page.

  13. Disable Reader Mode:

    • Toggle the switch to prevent users from enabling reader mode.

Example​

  • Disable Right Click:

    • Enabling this setting prevents users from right-clicking on any part of your site, protecting images and text from being copied via the context menu.

  • Disable Text Selection:

    • Enabling this setting stops users from highlighting text, ensuring that your written content cannot be easily copied.

  • Disable Print Screen & Detect Screen Capture:

    • Enabling this setting prevents users from taking screenshots, keeping your site’s visuals secure.

Practical Applications​

  • Content Protection:

    • Protect your site's content from being copied or accessed without permission, ensuring the integrity and exclusivity of your work.

  • Enhanced Security:

    • Use features like disabling developer tools and source view to make it harder for users to inspect or manipulate your site's code.

  • User Privacy:

    • Prevent WebRTC leaks and block clipboard access to safeguard user privacy and prevent unauthorized copying of content.

The General Settings feature provides a robust set of tools to secure your site’s content, ensuring it remains protected from unauthorized access and copying.
